Privacy Policy

Walk Focus · Last updated: May 20, 2026

Walk Focus is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

1. Information We Collect

Data How Collected Purpose
Location (GPS) Collected directly from device while app is in use Walking route calculation, distance measurement to destination, character movement tracking
Focus session history Stored on device (AsyncStorage) Displaying journey date, time, distance, and focus statistics
App usage events Firebase Analytics (Google) Anonymous statistics: journey start/complete/abandon, destination name, duration, etc.
Device identifier Automatically collected by Firebase SDK Anonymous usage analytics (not linked to any individual)

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, or any account credentials
  • Payment information
  • Contacts, camera, or microphone data
  • Background location (location is only accessed while the app is on screen)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Calculating walking routes and tracking real-time movement
  • Providing weekly and lifetime focus session statistics
  • Analyzing anonymous usage patterns to improve app features

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services. Please review each provider's privacy policy as well.

Service Provider Purpose
Google Maps SDK Google LLC Map display
OSRM Routing API OpenStreetMap Foundation Walking route calculation (coordinates only — no personal data transmitted)
Firebase Analytics Google LLC Anonymous app usage statistics

Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • On-device data (focus history, location, etc.): Automatically deleted when the app is uninstalled.
  • Firebase Analytics data: Retained for up to 14 months per Google's policy, then automatically deleted.
  • No collected data is transmitted to or stored on external servers operated by the developer.

6. Children's Privacy

Walk Focus is not directed at children under the age of 13. If we become aware that personal information has been collected from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.
